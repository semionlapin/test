#Biometrics #Security #Facial ID #Amazon

Colleen manaher shares how u.s. customs & border protection is using aws to improve travel & safety

U.S. Customs & Border Protection (CBP) is developing, testing, and promoting the use of facial recognition to improve the traveler experience and maintain U.S. national security. Colleen Manaher, Executive Director of Planning, Program Analysis, and Evaluation, Office of Field Operations, CBP, spoke at AWS re:Invent 2017 and described the agency’s journey to the cloud. Using technologies such as Amazon Rekognition, CBP’s goal is to eliminate the need for passports and boarding passes for international travelers, and to collaborate with sister agencies, such as TSA, to bring the same seamless experience to domestic travel. How can these types of technology facilitate MSC securities and identity related procedures and streamline the customer experience both on and off the ships?
